Who am I?

My name is Yan Portnoy.
I was born on August 11 1969 in Kishinev , Moldova, USSR. Attend school #53 and later #21. After I graduated from school I studied in the Technical school #1 and then in Kishinev Polytechnical Institute while I worked on the Schetmash Factory. I emigrated to the USA in 1991. Now I live in Atlanta, GA. In 2002, I graduated from GSU with a degree in Computer Sciences. My personal interest include Jewish history, a specially a period of theKhazar's Khanate; cooking and Computer Sciences.
